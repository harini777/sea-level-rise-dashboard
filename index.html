Understanding Coastal Vulnerability

Supporting coastal decision-making for urban zoning and evacuation planning

Sea level rise poses a significant threat to coastal communities worldwide. Rising oceans threaten infrastructure, homes, and ecosystems—demanding informed decision-making for urban planning and emergency preparedness. This dashboard provides a centralized platform to analyze risks, plan evacuations, and visualize trends to support policy and community resilience.

200M+ People at risk globally
~30 cm Rise projected by 2050
90% Coastal cities affected
$1T+ Infrastructure at risk

Dashboard Purpose

The Sea Level Rise Impact Dashboard is designed to support urban coastal zoning and evacuation planning by presenting risk analysis, planning insights, and visual summaries in a clear and user-friendly way. Decision-makers, planners, and community stakeholders can use this tool to understand exposure levels, prioritize resources, and develop actionable strategies.
